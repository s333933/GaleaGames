About

Galea AS has a goal of realizing a game concept that was started in 2012. Four students at Oslo Metropolitan University has taken on this job of realizing the game. The development of the game has a central role for the client's business. The most important thing is to make a game as engaging as possible, which will lay the foundation for more mini-games. The purpose is to give users an entertaining alternative to acquire knowledge of finance. Read more: the Galea Games homepage
